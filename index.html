About

What is Stackette HQ

Stackette HQ is a women-owned, bootstrapped studio building beautiful micro-SaaS apps from a feminine perspective.

The name Stackette comes from software “stacks” and SaaS — a nod to how we build lightweight, scalable tools that serve real people, not just vanity metrics.

We create design-first software that turns everyday ideas into profitable products, managing the full product lifecycle from spark to scale.

Each app is crafted with intention, validated with real users, and grown into a sustainable digital product — one elegant release at a time.
